Christmas Cornflake Wreath Cookies

Description

These cornflake wreaths are easy cookies to make with the kids during the holidays. Once people try them, they can't get enough!

Ingredients

  • waxed paper
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 cups miniature marshmallows
  • 1 teaspoon green food coloring, or as needed
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 cups cornflakes cereal
  • 1 (2.25 ounce) package cinnamon red hot candies

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Line a baking tray with waxed paper.

  3. Place marshmallows and butter in a microwave-safe bowl; microwave on high for 2 minutes.

    Stir, then microwave on high for 2 minutes more; stir.

  4. Mix in coloring, almond extract, and vanilla until the desired color is reached; gently stir in cornflakes to coat.

  5. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto the prepared tray and use lightly greased fingers to form wreath shapes; decorate with 3 red hots each.

  6. Allow cookies to cool and set, about 15 minutes; store in an airtight container.
